Criterion

Your AI copilot for debate.

Coming soon to the App Store

Intelligent Dashboard

Criterion tracks your upcoming debate schedule, monitors NSDA topic releases for LD, PF, Policy, and Big Questions, and organizes your recent prep files in a single, glanceable view.

Pro Flowing

Record speeches directly within the app to generate real-time transcriptions and AI-powered flows. Use automated hints based on your prep to find the perfect rebuttal or cross-ex question during the round.

Card Cutter

Research without the friction. Criterion’s AI-powered Card Cutter parses web articles and PDFs to extract key evidence, automatically cutting core cards and generating perfectly formatted citations in seconds.

Benchmarks

Evaluate your performance instantly. Criterion analyzes your speech transcripts to generate comprehensive ballots during practice rounds, complete with a Reason for Decision and speaker-specific feedback to refine your strategy. It also constantly analyzes RFDs and feedback from all your rounds and summarizes them in a central location so you have actionable items to improve.

Calendar Integration

Keep your schedule in sync. Criterion integrates seamlessly with the iOS Calendar app to automatically add your debate rounds and tournaments, complete with topics, sides, and locations.

Break Calculator

Predict your path to victory. The built-in Break Calculator analyzes tournament details and your current win-loss record to provide a statistical probability of advancing to elimination rounds.

Built for the Apple Ecosystem

Criterion is designed from the ground up for iOS and macOS. With native iCloud synchronization, your preparation files and round history are available on all your devices, protected by the same security you trust every day.
