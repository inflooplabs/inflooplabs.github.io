FAQ

How does Criterion sync my data?

Criterion automatically syncs your rounds, tournaments, and prep files across all your devices using your private iCloud container. No account sign in or creation is required.

What is the monthly AI token quota?

Each user receives a monthly allowance of 500,000 tokens for AI-powered features. Your usage resets automatically on the first day of every month.

Can I backup or export my data?

Yes. You can export your entire database or specific items as a JSON file through the Settings menu. This file can be used for manual backups or data transfers.

Can I sync rounds to my Calendar?

Yes. Criterion integrates with the iOS Calendar app to automatically sync your rounds and tournaments. You can choose a specific calendar for sync in Settings.

How does the Break Calculator work?

The Break Calculator uses your current record and tournament entry data to estimate your statistical probability of advancing to elimination rounds.

How are debate topics updated?

Criterion automatically monitors official NSDA topic releases for LD, PF, Policy, and Big Questions. New topics appear on your dashboard as soon as they are released, allowing you to start prep right away.
